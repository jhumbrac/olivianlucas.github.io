About Us

Bloomfield Maples Montessori, founded in 1983, offers an educational program with over 30 years of academic excellence. Our authentic Montessori classrooms bring out the best in children, allowing them to follow their own natural course of development, yet helping them to acquire valuable life skills. We are staffed by certified Montessori teachers and support staff that combine competence, warmth and years of experience. Call today to schedule a tour of our facility. We look forward to hearing from you!

Method

What is Montessori?
Dr. Maria Montessori profoundly felt that children have sensitive periods in which they are particularly able and interested in acquiring a certain knowledge or skill. Montessori believed the only way to learn is by "doing". At each stage materials were developed to correspond with the child's cognitive growth.
